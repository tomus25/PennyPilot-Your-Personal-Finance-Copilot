FAQ

Do I need to connect my bank?+

No. The MVP works fully offline with CSV uploads you export from your bank/credit card. A secure connection option may come later.

What does the MVP include?+

Auto‑categorization, subscription & recurring charge detection, bill/renewal email reminders, downloadable monthly report. Built within 10–15 days.

How will I be charged?+

Monthly subscription, cancel any time. Early‑bird price locks for life for first 1,000 users.

Is my data private?+

Yes. CSV files are processed locally in your browser for instant insights. If you opt into reminders, we only store the minimum required billing metadata.
